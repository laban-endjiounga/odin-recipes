Greek Steak


Designed by Freepik

Description

This Greek steak marinade, with 3 ingredients and a little salt and pepper, is all you need for flavorful steaks you won't soon forget.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh oregano
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose Greek seasoning, (such as Cavender'sÂ®)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk extra-virgin olive oil, oregano, Greek seasoning, salt, and pepper in a small bowl until smooth.
  3. Pour marinade over steaks, making sure to coat all sides. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours.
