Quick and Easy Baked Sweet Potato

Description

An alternative to traditional potatoes, sweet potatoes also offer many different possibilities : fries, gratin, mashed, crushed... For this quick and easy baked sweet potato recipe, just a few ingredients are all you need to enjoy a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into evenly sized wedges.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the sweet potato wedges with the olive oil, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper. Make sure the sweet potatoes are well coated with oil and spices.
  4. Place the sweet potato wedges on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, making sure not to crowd them so they cook evenly.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25 to 30 minutes, or until the sweet potatoes are tender inside and golden brown outside. Stir them halfway through cooking to ensure even cooking.
  6. Remove the sweet potatoes from the oven and serve hot. Garnish with chopped fresh herbs, such as parsley or cilantro, if desired.
